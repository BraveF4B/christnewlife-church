PROPHET. DAIRO OLAYEMI JEREMIAH

FOUNDER

Welcome to Christ's New Life Solution & Healing Church Worldwide. This is a place where God's power is real, miracles happen and lives are transformed. We believe that God has a solution to every problem and healing for every sickness. Come as you are and experience the love of God.

Ẹ káàbọ̀ sí Christ's New Life Solution & Healing Church Worldwide. Eyi ni ibi tí agbára Ọlọ́run ti ń ṣiṣẹ́ ní tòótọ́, ibi tí àwọn iṣẹ́ ìyanu ti ń ṣẹlẹ̀, tí a sì ti ń yí ìgbésí ayé àwọn ènìyàn padà fún rere. A gbàgbọ́ pé Ọlọ́run ní ojútùú fún gbogbo ìṣòro, àti ìwòsàn fún gbogbo àìsàn. Wá bí o ṣe rí, kí o sì ní ìrírí ìfẹ́ Ọlọ́run nínú ayé rẹ.

Salvation is the greatest gift God has ever given to mankind. No matter where you have been or what you have done, the grace of God is sufficient for you. If you confess with your mouth that Jesus is Lord and believe in your heart that God raised Him from the dead, you shall be saved. Today is your day of salvation — take that step of faith and let Jesus Christ transform your life forever.

Ìgbàlà ni ẹbùn tó tóbi jù lọ tí Ọlọ́run ti fún ẹ̀dá ènìyàn. Láìka ibiti o ti wà tàbí ohun tí o ti ṣe, oore-ọ̀fẹ́ Ọlọ́run tó tó fún ọ. Bí o bá jẹ́wọ́ pẹ̀lú ẹnu rẹ pé Jesu ni Olúwa, tí o sì gbàgbọ́ nínú ọkàn rẹ pé Ọlọ́run jí i dìde lárọ̀wọ́tọ́, a ó gbà ọ là. Òní ni ọjọ́ ìgbàlà rẹ — gba ìgbẹ̀kẹ̀lé ìgbàgbọ́ náà, kí Jesu Kristi sì yí ayé rẹ padà títí láé.

What We Believe

✝️
Salvation

We believe in the saving grace of Jesus Christ and that everyone can be saved through faith in Him.

🕊️
Holy Spirit

We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit and His gifts as available for believers today.

🙌
Divine Healing

We believe that God heals today just as He did in the Bible. Healing is available for all who believe.

📖
The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and the ultimate authority for Christian living.

🙏
Prayer

We believe in the power of prayer and that God answers the prayers of His people.

🌍
Evangelism

We are committed to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ to every nation, tribe and tongue.
