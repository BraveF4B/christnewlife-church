Our History

A Church Born
Out of Purpose

Christ's New Life Solution & Healing Church Worldwide was founded with a singular divine mandate to bring healing, restoration and the transforming power of Jesus Christ to every soul that walks through its doors.

Under the inspired leadership of Prophet. Dairo Olayemi Jeremiah ( P.K.A BABA FAYEGBAMI ), the church began on Tuesday 7th of September 2021 as a small gathering of believers in Ikirun, Osun State, and has since grown into a vibrant, Spirit-filled community touching thousands of lives across the region and beyond.

"We are not just a church, we are a place where broken people find wholeness, and hopeless hearts discover the limitless grace of God."

Today, the church stands as a beacon of faith - a place where prayer is answered, prophecy flows and every believer is equipped to fulfil their God-given destiny.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To raise a generation of Spirit-filled believers who walk boldly in the fullness of God's power — manifesting signs, wonders and healing to establish Christ's New Life as a global house of prayer, restoration and divine solutions for all nations.

Our Mission

To preach the undiluted Gospel of Jesus Christ with signs and wonders following; to provide healing — spiritual, physical and emotional to the broken-hearted; to disciple believers into maturity and to reach every community with the transforming love of God.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These principles are the foundation upon which everything we do is built.

01

Faith in God's Word

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the ultimate authority for faith, life and practice.

02

Prayer & Worship

We are a house of prayer. Fervent, Spirit-led prayer and heartfelt worship are at the core of our corporate and personal life.

03

Healing & Deliverance

We believe in the miraculous healing power of Jesus Christ and minister healing and deliverance to all who come in faith.

04

Discipleship

We are committed to growing believers to spiritual maturity through Bible teaching, mentoring and accountability.

05

Community & Love

We love one another and our community. We serve with compassion and create a family where every person is valued and belongs.

06

Evangelism & Missions

We are passionate about souls. We actively reach the lost — locally and globally with the saving message of Jesus Christ.
